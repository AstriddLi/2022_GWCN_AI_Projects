Number of Deaths Caused by Unsafe Water Usage in Worldwide

1990 - 2019 Data Analysis

Clean water is important to human health. Unsafe water sources can cause various kinds of health problems, and increase the risk for death. Lack of access to improved water sources is one of the major factors for infectious diseases and has been a worldwide challenge. This boxplot will display the annual number of death from 1990 to 2019 caused by unsafe water usage. Whisker of the boxplot is defined as 1.5 times the interquartile range. Each log graph can show 5 regions.
